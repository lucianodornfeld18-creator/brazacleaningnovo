What Is Maid Service in Minneola?

Professional maid service is recurring house cleaning by a consistent, assigned team — not a rotating crew of strangers. At Braza Cleaning, your Minneola maid service means the same professionals visit your home every scheduled visit, learn your preferences, and deliver results that improve over time.

What's Included in Every Visit

  • All bedrooms dusted, vacuumed, and mopped
  • All bathrooms fully scrubbed and sanitized
  • Kitchen counters, stovetop, sink, and appliance exteriors
  • Microwave interior cleaned
  • Living and dining areas dusted and floors cleaned
  • All trash emptied throughout the home
  • Light switches and door handles wiped
  • Mirrors and glass surfaces cleaned streak-free
  • Beds made and linens straightened
  • Baseboards dusted (deep clean visits)

Available Add-Ons for Minneola Clients

Inside oven (+$25–$45) · Inside refrigerator (+$20–$40) · Inside all cabinets · Lanai/patio cleaning · Window interior cleaning · Laundry folding
