Janitorial Services vs Commercial Cleaning — What's the Difference in Central Florida?

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

This guide covers everything Central Florida business owners and homeowners need to know about janitorial services vs commercial cleaning — what's the difference in central florida? in 2026. Whether you're in Orlando, Kissimmee, or Clermont or the surrounding area, understanding what professional cleaning involves, what it costs, and how to choose the right provider saves time and avoids costly mistakes.

What to Expect from Professional Cleaning in Central Florida

Central Florida's professional cleaning market has grown significantly over the past five years, driven by population growth across Orange, Osceola, Lake, Polk, and Seminole counties. The range of providers — from large national franchises to small locally-owned operations like Braza Cleaning Services — means quality and pricing vary considerably. Knowing what to look for is essential.

  • Licensed and insured — any professional cleaning operation in Florida should carry commercial liability insurance and be properly licensed. Ask for documentation before booking.
  • Consistent teams — whether residential or commercial, consistent assigned teams produce more reliable results than rotating different people through your space.
  • Transparent flat-rate pricing — professional cleaning services should quote a flat rate per visit, not open-ended hourly billing that makes costs unpredictable.
  • Satisfaction guarantee — a confident cleaning company stands behind its work. If something isn't right, they return and fix it at no charge.
  • Clear scope of service — know exactly what is and isn't included before the first visit. Surprises in scope lead to budget surprises.

Pricing Benchmarks for Central Florida (2026)

Market rates for professional cleaning services throughout Central Florida in 2026 generally fall within these ranges:

  • Residential standard cleaning (2BR home): $120–$180 per visit
  • Residential standard cleaning (3BR home): $130–$200 per visit
  • Residential deep cleaning (3BR home): $240–$380
  • Move-out cleaning (3BR home): $260–$390
  • Commercial office cleaning (1,000 sq ft, weekly): $150–$280 per visit
  • Vacation rental turnover (3BR): $165–$255 per turnover

Prices vary based on specific location within Central Florida, property condition, and the specific scope of services included. Always get a written quote before committing to any cleaning service.

How to Choose the Right Cleaning Service in Central Florida

The most reliable way to evaluate a cleaning service in Central Florida before committing to a recurring arrangement:

  • Ask for references from clients in your specific city or neighborhood
  • Read Google reviews specifically mentioning reliability and consistency — not just quality of the first visit
  • Ask directly: "What happens if my scheduled cleaner is sick or unavailable?"
  • Verify insurance in writing — request a certificate of insurance, not just a verbal confirmation
  • Start with a one-time cleaning to evaluate before committing to recurring service
