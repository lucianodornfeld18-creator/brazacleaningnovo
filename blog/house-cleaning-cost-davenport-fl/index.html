How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Davenport FL has grown from a quiet Polk County town into one of Central Florida's most sought-after communities — and that growth has brought a wide range of house cleaning services into the market. Whether you're in Providence, Solterra, or the established neighborhoods near Posner Park, this guide covers real 2026 pricing for residential house cleaning in Davenport.

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL (2026)

Standard residential cleaning in Davenport covers all bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen, living areas, and floors. Davenport homes tend to be newer and larger than many comparable Central Florida cities — Providence and similar communities feature 2,000–3,500 sq ft homes that are spacious but also well-designed for efficient cleaning:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $87–$127
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $117–$177
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $127–$197
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $147–$217
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $157–$337+

Davenport's position at the Polk-Osceola border means many residents commute to Kissimmee, Celebration, or the theme park corridor — making reliable recurring cleaning a practical necessity for busy households.

Recurring House Cleaning Cost in Davenport (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)

Remote workers and commuting families in Ridge Top, Providence, and Solterra have fueled demand for bi-weekly residential cleaning in Davenport. Consistent scheduling is a priority in this market:

  • Weekly service (2BR home): $117–$177 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (2BR home): $117–$177 per visit
  • Monthly service (2BR home): $117–$177 per visit
  • Weekly service (3BR home): $127–$197 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (3BR home): $127–$197 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (4BR home): $147–$217 per visit

Deep Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL

Deep cleaning is a strong seller in Davenport because many homes were originally built as vacation rentals and later converted to primary residences — these properties often need a thorough reset before starting recurring service:

  • 2-bedroom home: $177–$257
  • 3-bedroom home: $237–$357
  • 4-bedroom home: $307–$447
  • 5+ bedroom home: $397–$597+

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL

Davenport's fast-growing residential base means move-out cleaning is in high demand — particularly as families upgrade from starter homes near Posner Village to larger builds in newer Davenport developments:

  • 1-bedroom / 1-bathroom: $197–$287
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $257–$377
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $337–$477
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom: $417–$557
  • Large home (5+ BR): $497–$707+

What Factors Affect House Cleaning Prices in Davenport?

  • Home size — Davenport's newer homes average larger square footage than older Central Florida markets
  • Prior use as vacation rental — homes transitioning from short-term rental use may require a deep-clean first visit
  • Pets — many Davenport families have dogs; pet hair on tile and carpet adds time
  • Screened pool enclosures — common amenity in Davenport; pool deck cleaning is quoted separately
  • Frequency — recurring clients save 10–20% per visit

How to Evaluate House Cleaning Quotes in Davenport

  • Licensed and insured — Davenport's growth has attracted many informal operations; always verify
  • Flat-rate pricing — hourly quotes can balloon unpredictably, especially in larger Davenport homes
  • Background-checked staff — essential for families in gated communities like Providence
  • Consistent team assignment — the best companies send the same team every visit
  • Satisfaction guarantee — look for companies that will re-clean at no charge if you're not happy
