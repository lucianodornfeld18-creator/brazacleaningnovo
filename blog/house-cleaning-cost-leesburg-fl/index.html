How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Leesburg FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Leesburg FL is one of Lake County's most historic cities — and one undergoing genuine revitalization. With the Harris Chain of Lakes drawing retirees, boaters, and remote workers, demand for professional house cleaning in Leesburg has grown steadily. This guide covers real 2026 pricing for all service types in Leesburg.

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Leesburg FL (2026)

Leesburg homes range from downtown bungalows near Venetian Gardens to larger lakefront properties on Lake Harris and newer builds in Plantation at Leesburg. Standard pricing reflects this range:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $85–$125
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $115–$175
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $125–$195
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $145–$215
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $155–$335+

Leesburg's historic downtown district includes some of Central Florida's oldest homes — properties with hardwood floors, plaster walls, and vintage fixtures that require careful handling by experienced cleaners.

Recurring House Cleaning Cost in Leesburg (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)

Leesburg's active adult communities — particularly Plantation at Leesburg and Silver Lake — have a strong appetite for monthly or bi-weekly recurring cleaning where reliability is paramount:

  • Weekly service (2BR home): $115–$175 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (2BR home): $115–$175 per visit
  • Monthly service (2BR home): $115–$175 per visit
  • Weekly service (3BR home): $125–$195 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (3BR home): $125–$195 per visit

Deep Cleaning Cost in Leesburg FL

  • 2-bedroom home: $175–$255
  • 3-bedroom home: $235–$355
  • 4-bedroom home: $305–$445
  • 5+ bedroom home: $395–$595+

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning Cost in Leesburg FL

  • 1-bedroom / 1-bathroom: $195–$285
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $255–$375
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $335–$475
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom: $415–$555

What Factors Affect House Cleaning Prices in Leesburg?

  • Home age — historic downtown Leesburg homes have surfaces that require more careful cleaning
  • Lakefront properties — homes on Lake Harris or Lake Griffin may have boat docks and screened lanais quoted separately
  • Frequency — recurring clients save 10–20% per visit
  • Community type — active adult communities often have specific access procedures that affect scheduling

How to Evaluate House Cleaning Quotes in Leesburg

  • Licensed and insured — always verify before booking
  • Reliability — Leesburg clients value consistent scheduling above almost everything else
  • Flat-rate pricing — get a per-visit flat rate, not an hourly estimate
  • Satisfaction guarantee — look for a re-clean commitment
