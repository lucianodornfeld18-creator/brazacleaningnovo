Airbnb Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot in Orlando FL (2026): Does Square Footage Actually Matter?

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Many Orlando Airbnb hosts assume cleaning is priced per square foot — like painting or flooring. It's a logical assumption, but it's not how vacation rental cleaning actually works in this market. Square footage matters, but it's not the primary driver. This guide explains what actually determines cleaning cost for Orlando vacation rentals in 2026, and why two 2,000 sq ft homes can have very different cleaning prices.

Does Square Footage Determine Airbnb Cleaning Cost in Orlando?

The short answer: square footage is a factor, but bathrooms, amenities, and property complexity matter more. A 2,000 sq ft home with 2 bathrooms cleans faster than a 2,000 sq ft home with 4 bathrooms and a game room — even though they're the same size.

For reference, here's how per-square-foot pricing would look if it were the standard in Orlando:

  • 800 sq ft condo at $0.12–$0.18/sq ft = $96–$144
  • 1,500 sq ft home at $0.10–$0.15/sq ft = $150–$225
  • 2,500 sq ft home at $0.08–$0.13/sq ft = $200–$325
  • 3,500 sq ft resort home at $0.07–$0.11/sq ft = $245–$385

These ranges roughly align with actual market rates — but they can be misleading because they don't account for the variables that actually drive price.

What Actually Determines Cleaning Cost in Orlando Vacation Rentals

Professional cleaning services in Orlando price based on time and complexity, not square footage. The key variables:

  • Number of bathrooms — each bathroom adds 20–35 minutes of cleaning time and is the single biggest cost driver after bedroom count
  • Pool and outdoor spaces — pool decks, covered lanais, and outdoor kitchens add 20–40 minutes per turnover
  • Specialty rooms — game rooms, home theaters, and themed bedrooms (common in Orlando vacation rentals near convention center and Disney) add time independently of square footage
  • Guest behavior and checkout condition — open floor plans in Orlando vacation rentals show mess more visibly and require more surface area coverage
  • Laundry volume — a 3BR/3BA Orlando home generates more laundry than a 3BR/2BA even at the same square footage

Actual Orlando Airbnb Cleaning Prices by Property Type (2026)

  • Studio / 1BR condo (500–900 sq ft): $90–$135
  • 2BR / 2BA home (900–1,400 sq ft): $125–$180
  • 3BR / 2BA home (1,400–2,000 sq ft): $140–$200
  • 3BR / 3BA home (1,600–2,200 sq ft): $155–$225
  • 4BR / 3BA home (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $160–$240
  • 5BR resort home (2,800–4,000 sq ft): $180–$290
  • 6–8BR large resort (4,000–6,000 sq ft): $230–$430

When Square Footage Does Matter in Orlando

Square footage becomes relevant in a few specific scenarios:

  • Open-concept great rooms — 800–1,200 sq ft of combined kitchen/dining/living space takes longer to fully wipe down than a compartmentalized floor plan of the same size
  • Luxury vacation rentals in Dr. Phillips and Windermere — larger homes with 20+ ft ceilings, large tile floors, and extensive countertop surfaces have more linear feet of surfaces to clean
  • Post-construction or deep cleaning — when cleaning every surface including inside cabinets and behind appliances, sq ft matters more directly
  • Very large properties (5,000+ sq ft) — at this scale, the floor area alone becomes a significant time factor

How to Get an Accurate Quote for Your Orlando Property

Don't provide square footage alone when requesting a cleaning quote in Orlando. Give your cleaner:

  • Exact bedroom and bathroom count
  • Whether you have a pool, game room, or home theater
  • Expected checkout condition (standard guests vs. large groups)
  • Whether laundry service is included or separate
  • Frequency of turnover (recurring vs. one-time)

A professional service will use this information to give you a flat rate per turnover — which is the most transparent and predictable pricing model in this market.

FAQ: Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot in Orlando FL

Is Airbnb cleaning in Orlando priced by square footage?

Not typically. Professional Orlando vacation rental cleaning services price by property type, bedroom/bathroom count, and amenities — not square footage. Per-sq-ft estimates are useful for ballpark budgeting but shouldn't replace a flat-rate quote from your cleaner.

Why does a smaller home sometimes cost more to clean than a larger one in Orlando?

A smaller home with more bathrooms, a pool, and a game room can cost more to clean than a larger home with a simple layout and fewer bathrooms. The number of high-complexity spaces matters more than total square footage.

What's a reasonable cleaning rate per square foot for an Orlando vacation rental?

Ballpark: $0.08–$0.18 per square foot depending on property complexity, amenities, and whether laundry is included. Use this only as a rough estimate — always get a flat rate by property for accurate budgeting.
