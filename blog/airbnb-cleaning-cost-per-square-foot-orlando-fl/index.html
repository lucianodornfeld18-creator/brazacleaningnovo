Airbnb Cleaning Cost Per Square Foot in Orlando FL (2026): Does Square Footage Actually Matter?

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Many Orlando Airbnb hosts assume cleaning is priced per square foot — like painting or flooring. It's a logical assumption, but it's not how vacation rental cleaning actually works in this market. Square footage matters, but it's not the primary driver. This guide explains what actually determines cleaning cost for Orlando vacation rentals in 2026, and why two 2,000 sq ft homes can have very different cleaning prices.

Does Square Footage Determine Airbnb Cleaning Cost in Orlando?

👉 Related: Airbnb Cleaning in Orlando, FL →

The short answer: square footage is a factor, but bathrooms, amenities, and property complexity matter more. A 2,000 sq ft home with 2 bathrooms cleans faster than a 2,000 sq ft home with 4 bathrooms and a game room — even though they're the same size.

For reference, here's how per-square-foot pricing would look if it were the standard in Orlando:

  • 800 sq ft condo at $0.12–$0.18/sq ft = $96–$144
  • 1,500 sq ft home at $0.10–$0.15/sq ft = $150–$225
  • 2,500 sq ft home at $0.08–$0.13/sq ft = $200–$325
  • 3,500 sq ft resort home at $0.07–$0.11/sq ft = $245–$385

These ranges roughly align with actual market rates — but they can be misleading because they don't account for the variables that actually drive price.

What Actually Determines Cleaning Cost in Orlando Vacation Rentals

Professional cleaning services in Orlando price based on time and complexity, not square footage. The key variables:

  • Number of bathrooms — each bathroom adds 20–35 minutes of cleaning time and is the single biggest cost driver after bedroom count
  • Pool and outdoor spaces — pool decks, covered lanais, and outdoor kitchens add 20–40 minutes per turnover
  • Specialty rooms — game rooms, home theaters, and themed bedrooms (common in Orlando vacation rentals near convention center and Disney) add time independently of square footage
  • Guest behavior and checkout condition — open floor plans in Orlando vacation rentals show mess more visibly and require more surface area coverage
  • Laundry volume — a 3BR/3BA Orlando home generates more laundry than a 3BR/2BA even at the same square footage
