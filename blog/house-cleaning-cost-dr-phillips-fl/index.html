How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Dr. Phillips FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

Dr. Phillips is one of Orlando's most prestigious unincorporated communities — a ZIP code where Restaurant Row meets Bay Hill, and where homeowners consistently expect the highest standard of professional cleaning. This guide covers real 2026 pricing for house cleaning in Dr. Phillips FL (32819).

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Dr. Phillips FL (2026)

Dr. Phillips homes range from executive townhomes near Sand Lake Road to custom lakefront estates in Bay Hill and the Turkey Lake area. Pricing in 32819 reflects the market's premium tier:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $100–$140
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $130–$190
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $140–$210
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $160–$230
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $170–$350+

Bay Hill properties and custom homes near Turkey Lake often exceed 4,000 square feet with multiple living areas, formal dining rooms, and spa bathrooms — all of which require more time and detailed attention than standard floor plans.
