How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Ocoee FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Ocoee is a growing Orange County city where established neighborhoods meet new master-planned communities — and where demand for professional house cleaning has expanded steadily alongside the population. From Forest Lake and Lake Olympia to the West Oaks corridor, this guide covers real 2026 house cleaning prices in Ocoee FL.

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Ocoee FL (2026)

Ocoee homes range from modest 3-bedroom builds in the Forest Lake area to larger newer homes in the Olympia and West Oaks developments. Standard cleaning rates reflect this mix:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $89–$129
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $119–$179
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $129–$199
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $149–$219
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $159–$339+

Recurring House Cleaning Cost in Ocoee (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)

Many Ocoee families commute to downtown Orlando, Winter Garden, or the International Drive corridor — making bi-weekly or weekly recurring cleaning a practical time-saver:

  • Weekly service (2BR home): $119–$179 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (2BR home): $119–$179 per visit
  • Monthly service (2BR home): $119–$179 per visit
  • Weekly service (3BR home): $129–$199 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (3BR home): $129–$199 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (4BR home): $149–$219 per visit

Deep Cleaning Cost in Ocoee FL

  • 2-bedroom home: $179–$259
  • 3-bedroom home: $239–$359
  • 4-bedroom home: $309–$449
  • 5+ bedroom home: $399–$599+

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning Cost in Ocoee FL

  • 1-bedroom / 1-bathroom: $199–$289
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $259–$379
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $339–$479
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom: $419–$559
  • Large home (5+ BR): $499–$709+

What Factors Affect House Cleaning Prices in Ocoee?

  • Home size and layout — West Oaks area homes tend to have larger open-plan living spaces that take more floor and surface time
  • Pool enclosures — screened pool decks are common in Ocoee; these are typically quoted as add-ons
  • Pets — a significant factor for Ocoee families; pet hair on tile adds time
  • Frequency — recurring clients save 10–20% per visit vs. one-time rates
  • Home age — older Forest Lake homes may have tile grout and older kitchen surfaces that need extra attention

How to Evaluate House Cleaning Quotes in Ocoee

  • Licensed and insured — verify before booking any team into your home
  • Flat-rate quote — ask for a per-visit flat rate, not an open-ended hourly estimate
  • Background checks — essential for family homes in Ocoee
  • Consistent team — the same cleaners every visit is worth prioritizing
  • Satisfaction guarantee — look for a re-clean commitment if you're not satisfied
