How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Maitland FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Maitland is one of Central Florida's most sought-after small cities — a lakeside community bordered by Winter Park and Altamonte Springs where homeowners have exacting expectations for professional cleaning. This guide covers real 2026 pricing for house cleaning in Maitland FL.

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Maitland FL (2026)

Maitland homes range from mid-century properties near Lake Lily to newer executive builds in Dommerich Hills and the Maitland Exchange corridor. Pricing reflects the city's upscale positioning:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $95–$135
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $125–$185
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $135–$205
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $155–$225
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $165–$345+

Maitland's proximity to AdventHealth and the Maitland Center business district means many residents are healthcare professionals and executives who rely on consistent, detail-oriented cleaning services.

  • Bi-weekly service (2BR home): $125–$185 per visit
  • Monthly service (2BR home): $125–$185 per visit
  • Weekly service (3BR home): $135–$205 per visit

What Factors Affect House Cleaning Prices in Maitland?

  • Specialty surfaces — Dommerich Hills and lakefront properties often feature hardwood floors and stone countertops requiring specific care
  • Lake access properties — homes on Lake Lily or Lake Maitland may have dock areas and outdoor entertaining spaces quoted separately
  • Mid-century construction — some Maitland homes have unique architectural details that take more time
  • High-frequency demand — Maitland's professional demographic often books weekly service

How to Evaluate House Cleaning Quotes in Maitland

  • Licensed and insured with strong coverage — Maitland home values demand adequate liability protection
  • Background-checked employees — non-negotiable in this market
  • Consistent team assignment — weekly-service clients especially benefit from the same team each visit
