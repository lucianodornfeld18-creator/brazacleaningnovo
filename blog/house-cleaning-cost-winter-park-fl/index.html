How Much Does House Cleaning Cost in Winter Park FL? (2026 Pricing Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Winter Park is one of Central Florida's most desirable cities — and one where homeowners have the highest expectations for professional cleaning. From the historic estates along the Chain of Lakes to the condos near Park Avenue and Rollins College, this guide covers real 2026 pricing for house cleaning in Winter Park FL.

Standard House Cleaning Cost in Winter Park FL (2026)

Winter Park homes range from charming 1950s bungalows in the College Quarter to multi-story lakefront properties in Windsong and Lake Knowles. Standard cleaning pricing reflects the city's premium positioning in the Central Florida market:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom (up to 800 sq ft): $98–$138
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom (800–1,400 sq ft): $128–$188
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom (1,400–2,200 sq ft): $138–$208
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom (2,200–3,000 sq ft): $158–$228
  • 5+ bedroom / large homes (3,000+ sq ft): $168–$348+

Older Winter Park homes near Hannibal Square and Park Avenue often have hardwood floors, ornate fixtures, and custom cabinetry that require careful attention — a quality professional team handles these surfaces differently than standard tile and laminate.

Recurring House Cleaning Cost in Winter Park (Weekly, Bi-Weekly, Monthly)

Winter Park homeowners tend to prioritize consistency and quality over price — the most common feedback from our Winter Park clients is that they value the same team every visit and detailed attention to specialty surfaces:

  • Weekly service (2BR home): $128–$188 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (2BR home): $128–$188 per visit
  • Monthly service (2BR home): $128–$188 per visit
  • Weekly service (3BR home): $138–$208 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (3BR home): $138–$208 per visit
  • Bi-weekly service (4BR home): $158–$228 per visit

Deep Cleaning Cost in Winter Park FL

Winter Park's older homes — particularly those built in the 1960s–1980s near the Morse Museum and Rollins College — benefit from annual deep cleans that address grout, cabinet interiors, and hard-to-reach areas that standard cleaning doesn't cover:

  • 2-bedroom home: $188–$268
  • 3-bedroom home: $248–$368
  • 4-bedroom home: $318–$458
  • 5+ bedroom home: $408–$608+

Move-In / Move-Out Cleaning Cost in Winter Park FL

Winter Park's active real estate market — driven by Rollins faculty, business executives, and families relocating from out of state — generates consistent demand for move-out cleaning at high standards:

  • 1-bedroom / 1-bathroom: $208–$298
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $268–$388
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $348–$488
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom: $428–$568
  • Large home (5+ BR): $508–$718+

What Factors Affect House Cleaning Prices in Winter Park?

  • Specialty surfaces — hardwood floors, marble countertops, and custom cabinetry require specific products and techniques
  • Home age and architecture — older historic homes near Park Avenue have intricate moldings and surfaces that take longer
  • Lakefront properties — homes on Lake Osceola, Lake Virginia, or Lake Maitland often have dock areas, boathouses, or large outdoor entertaining spaces quoted separately
  • Interior size vs. square footage — vaulted ceilings and open layouts in Lake Knowles area homes add cleaning complexity
  • Pets — particularly relevant for larger homes with multiple pets

How to Evaluate House Cleaning Quotes in Winter Park

  • Experience with specialty surfaces — not every cleaner knows how to handle Brazilian cherry hardwood or Carrera marble without causing damage
  • Licensed and insured with adequate coverage — Winter Park homes have significant value; verify liability limits
  • Background-checked, vetted employees — non-negotiable for high-value residential properties
  • References from Winter Park clients — a company that regularly cleans in Winter Park will have references from the area
  • Flat-rate written quote — protects you from unexpected charges in larger or more complex homes
