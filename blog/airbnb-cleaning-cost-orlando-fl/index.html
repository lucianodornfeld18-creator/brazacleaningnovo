Orlando's vacation rental inventory spans urban condos near the convention center, executive properties for corporate travelers, lakefront homes for upscale guests, and resort-adjacent pools homes competing with Kissimmee and Davenport. Pricing reflects this variety:

What Is Included in an Orlando Airbnb Turnover?

A professional Airbnb turnover in Orlando covers everything guests see, touch, and use:

  • All bedrooms: linens changed and beds made hotel-style, surfaces wiped, floors vacuumed and mopped
  • All bathrooms: full scrub and sanitization, mirrors polished, amenities restocked
  • Kitchen: countertops, stovetop, sink, appliance exteriors, microwave, dishwasher emptied
  • Living and dining areas: all surfaces dusted and wiped, floors cleaned
  • Pool deck: swept and outdoor furniture wiped (where applicable)
  • Trash removed from all areas
  • Final photo walkthrough documentation

Add-ons: inside oven (+$25–$45), inside refrigerator (+$20–$40), laundry service (+$15–$30/load), concierge restocking.

Deep Turnover Cleaning Cost in Orlando FL

Deep turnovers are essential after stays of 7+ nights and recommended monthly for high-volume Orlando properties:

  • 3-bedroom home: $185–$285
  • 4-bedroom home: $245–$365
  • 5-bedroom property: $305–$455
  • 6–8 bedroom large property: $405–$605+

What Factors Affect Airbnb Cleaning Costs in Orlando?

  • Property type — a Dr. Phillips lakefront home and an I-Drive condo have completely different cleaning requirements and access logistics
  • Stay length and guest profile — convention business travelers (2-night stays) vs. Disney families (7 nights) leave very different properties
  • Building access — condos in Rosen-area high-rises and gated Windermere communities have access procedures that affect scheduling
  • Pool and outdoor areas — prevalent in Dr. Phillips and Windermere vacation rentals; pool deck is standard scope for those properties
  • Turnover frequency — Orlando hosts with 2–3 night average stays need teams available 5–6 days per week
  • Location within Orlando — properties near Lake Nona (southeast) or Winter Garden border (west) may be farther from service hubs

How to Choose an Airbnb Cleaning Service in Orlando FL

  • Experience with your property type — condo cleaning in a high-rise has different logistics than a pool home in Dr. Phillips
