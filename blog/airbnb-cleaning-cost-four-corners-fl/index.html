Airbnb Turnover Cleaning Prices in Four Corners FL (2026)

Windsor Hills is particularly notable in the Four Corners market — this gated community features security checkpoints, resort amenities including a clubhouse and water park, and a concentration of 5–8 bedroom homes that host large family groups. Turnovers here require teams experienced with resort-style properties.

What Is Included in a Four Corners Airbnb Turnover?

Standard Four Corners Airbnb turnover cleaning includes:

  • All bedrooms: linens changed hotel-style, beds made, surfaces wiped, mirrors cleaned, floors vacuumed and mopped
  • All bathrooms: full scrub of toilet, sink, shower, and tub; mirrors polished; towels replaced; amenities restocked
  • Kitchen: countertops, stovetop, sink, appliance exteriors, microwave interior, dishwasher emptied
  • Living and dining areas: all surfaces dusted, floors cleaned
  • Pool deck: swept, patio furniture wiped and arranged — standard for virtually all Four Corners vacation homes
  • Trash removed from all rooms
  • Final walkthrough and photo documentation

Add-ons: inside oven (+$25–$45), inside refrigerator (+$20–$40), laundry service (+$15–$30/load), game room setup, and consumable restocking.
