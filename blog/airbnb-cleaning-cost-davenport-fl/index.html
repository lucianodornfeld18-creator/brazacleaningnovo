How Much Does Airbnb Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL? (2026 Guide)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Davenport FL sits at the center of Central Florida's most active vacation rental zone — and Airbnb hosts here deal with some of the most demanding turnover conditions in the region. From ChampionsGate to Providence and Solterra Resort, this guide covers real 2026 pricing for professional Airbnb cleaning in Davenport, specifically for the resort-style pool homes that dominate this market.

Airbnb Turnover Cleaning Prices in Davenport FL (2026)

Davenport vacation rentals are primarily large resort homes with private pools, game rooms, and themed bedrooms — built specifically for the short-term rental market serving Disney World, Universal, and LEGOLAND guests. Turnover pricing reflects the complexity of these properties:

  • Studio / 1-bedroom condo: $88–$133 per turnover
  • 2-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $123–$173 per turnover
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bathroom: $133–$193 per turnover
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bathroom: $153–$223 per turnover
  • 5-bedroom resort home: $163–$423 per turnover
  • 6–8 bedroom large resort home: $203–$503 per turnover

ChampionsGate properties specifically — where 8 and 10-bedroom resort homes with two-story water slides and arcade rooms are common — typically fall at the high end of these ranges. A 10-bedroom mega-property with full resort amenities can run $600–$900+ per turnover.

What Is Included in a Davenport Airbnb Turnover?

A professional Airbnb turnover in Davenport must meet the expectations of Disney-area guests who are paying premium nightly rates for resort-quality accommodations. Standard turnover includes:

  • All bedrooms: linens changed and beds made hotel-style, all surfaces wiped, mirrors polished, floors vacuumed and mopped
  • All bathrooms: toilet, sink, shower, tub fully scrubbed; mirrors polished; towels replaced; all guest amenities restocked
  • Kitchen: counters, stovetop, sink, appliance exteriors, microwave interior, dishwasher emptied
  • Living and dining areas: all surfaces dusted and wiped, floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Pool deck: swept and patio furniture wiped — essential for every Davenport vacation rental
  • Game rooms: all surfaces wiped, equipment checked and organized
  • Trash removed from all areas
  • Final photo walkthrough

Add-ons typically include: inside oven (+$25–$45), inside refrigerator (+$20–$40), laundry service (+$15–$30/load), home theater cleaning, and restocking of guest consumables.

Deep Turnover Cleaning Cost in Davenport FL

After 7+ night stays — which are extremely common in Davenport vacation rentals, where families book for Disney week — a deep turnover is essential to maintain property ratings. Deep cleaning includes inside all cabinets, grout scrubbing, full bathroom descaling, and behind-appliance cleaning:

  • 3-bedroom home: $183–$283
  • 4-bedroom home: $243–$363
  • 5-bedroom resort home: $303–$453
  • 6–8 bedroom large resort home: $403–$603+

What Factors Affect Airbnb Cleaning Costs in Davenport?

  • Property size and amenities — a 5BR ChampionsGate home with water slide, game room, and home theater is a fundamentally different scope than a standard 3BR pool home
  • Pool and spa — virtually every Davenport vacation rental has a pool; pool deck cleaning is a standard expectation, not an add-on in this market
  • Game room and specialty spaces — arcade rooms, themed bedrooms, and home theaters add 20–45 minutes per turnover
  • Guest departure condition — large family groups and multi-family bookings common in Davenport can leave properties in significantly varying condition
  • Volume and frequency — Davenport hosts with multiple properties or high-volume bookings receive better flat-rate pricing
  • Laundry volume — 6+ bedroom homes generate substantial linen volume; laundry is the biggest variable cost in Davenport turnovers

How Airbnb Cleaning Costs Affect Your Davenport Listing

Davenport is a hyper-competitive Airbnb market — thousands of similar properties compete on the same search pages. Cleanliness rating is one of the few factors that meaningfully differentiates your listing:

  • Airbnb's algorithm weights cleanliness ratings heavily in search ranking; properties consistently below 4.8 cleanliness see measurable booking decline
  • Davenport guests often book for Disney weeks and expect resort-quality cleanliness — these guests are not forgiving in reviews
  • A cleaning fee of 8–15% of the nightly rate is well within guest expectations for Davenport vacation homes; $200/night properties can absorb a $25–$40 cleaning fee without suppressing bookings
  • Consistent professional turnover typically generates ROI through higher cleanliness scores, better reviews, and repeat bookings within 6–12 months

How to Choose an Airbnb Cleaning Service in Davenport FL

Davenport's vacation rental market has attracted dozens of cleaning companies — from large regional operators to solo cleaners working part-time. Here's how to identify a professional service:

  • Resort property experience — cleaning a 10-bedroom ChampionsGate home is completely different from cleaning a 3BR house; verify experience with large resort properties
  • Same-day turnover capability — Davenport properties with 10am checkout and 4pm checkin need a team that can complete a full turnover in a 5-hour window
  • Photo documentation every turnover — protects you from guest damage disputes and provides quality accountability
  • 7-day availability including Florida holidays — Davenport is busiest on weekends and school breaks; your cleaner must be available
  • Licensed and insured — essential before allowing any team into a property you own or manage
  • Damage reporting protocol — a professional service notifies you immediately of any damage, stains, or missing items
