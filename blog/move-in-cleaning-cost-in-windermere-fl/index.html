How Much Does Move-In Cleaning Cost in Windermere, FL? (2026)

Move-in cleaning is a one-time deep clean of an empty (or nearly empty) home before you unpack. Because the cleaner reaches places that are normally blocked by furniture — inside every cabinet, behind appliances, and along every baseboard — it costs more than a standard recurring visit. Windermere's larger luxury homes also push the range higher than the Orlando metro average. Here are current 2026 move-in cleaning rates by home size:

Home sizeMove-in clean (per visit)
Condo / 1-2 BR$150 - $230
3 BR$240 - $330
4-5 BR$340 - $480
6+ BR / estate$500 - $780

The Braza Cleaning minimum for any visit is $120, so even a small condo move-in starts there. Most Windermere homeowners moving into a 3 to 5 bedroom home pay somewhere in the $240 to $480 range. The final number depends on square footage, condition, and add-ons like interior windows or inside-the-oven detailing.

What's the Difference Between Move-In, Standard, and Deep Cleaning?

People often confuse these three services, but they cover very different scopes and price points. A standard clean maintains an already-tidy home, a deep clean resets a lived-in home, and a move-in clean targets an empty home so it's spotless before your furniture arrives. Here's how they compare in Windermere:

ServiceBest forTypical 3 BR price
Standard cleanMaintained, furnished home$140 - $190
Deep cleanLived-in home, first-time reset$240 - $360
Move-in cleanEmpty home before unpacking$240 - $330

The big difference with a move-in clean is access: with no furniture in the way, the team cleans the insides of cabinets, drawers, closets, and appliances that a standard visit can't reach. That's exactly what you want before stocking your kitchen and closets in a new Windermere home.

What Affects Your Move-In Cleaning Price in Windermere?

Two homes the same size can land at different prices. These are the biggest factors that move your quote up or down:

  • Square footage: Windermere homes in Keene's Pointe, Isleworth, and the Reserve at Belmere are often 3,000+ sq ft, which adds labor and time.
  • Condition: A freshly vacated home that was kept tidy costs less than one with built-up grime, pet residue, or post-renovation dust.
  • Inside appliances: Detailing inside the oven and refrigerator is included in a move-in clean, but heavy baked-on buildup can add time.
  • Add-ons: Interior window glass, wall spot-cleaning, garage sweep-out, or screened lanai cleaning.
  • Timeline: Same-day or next-day bookings during peak moving season (late spring through summer) can carry a priority surcharge.

Whatever the variables, Braza confirms a flat, all-in quote before the job — no surprise hourly creep — and never quotes below the $120 minimum.

What's Included in a Windermere Move-In Clean?

A Braza move-in clean is room-by-room and built for an empty home. A typical scope includes:

  • Kitchen: inside and outside of all cabinets and drawers, countertops, inside the oven, inside and outside the refrigerator, dishwasher exterior, sink, and backsplash
  • Bathrooms: showers, tubs, toilets, vanities, inside cabinets and drawers, mirrors, and tile scrub
  • All rooms: baseboards, light switches, door frames, ceiling fans, vents, and window sills wiped
  • Closets: shelves and floors wiped and vacuumed
  • Floors: every floor vacuumed and mopped, including closets and pantry
  • Detail work: cobweb removal, interior glass on request, and spot-cleaning of marks on walls

Because the home is empty, the team can be thorough top-to-bottom — the result is a home that's genuinely ready for boxes the same day.

Is Move-In Cleaning Worth It Before You Unpack?

For most Windermere buyers and renters, yes. The previous owner's idea of "clean" rarely matches yours, and the empty-home window is the only time you can easily clean inside cabinets, closets, and behind where appliances sit. Once your furniture and boxes are in, reaching those spots becomes a weekend-long chore.

It's also the most cost-efficient moment to deep clean. You're paying one flat fee for a complete reset instead of chipping away at it for weeks. Many clients pair a move-in clean for the new home with a move-out clean on the home they're leaving — useful for getting a rental deposit back or prepping a sale.

How Far in Advance Should You Book Move-In Cleaning?

Aim to schedule your move-in clean for the day after closing or the day you get keys, and before the moving truck arrives. In practice that means booking 5 to 10 days ahead during Central Florida's busy moving season (April through August), when calendars fill quickly.

If your timeline is tight, Braza also offers same-day and next-day move-in cleaning in Windermere when crews are available. Call or text early in the day for the best chance at a same-day slot. To lock in your preferred date, request a quote as soon as your closing date is confirmed.
