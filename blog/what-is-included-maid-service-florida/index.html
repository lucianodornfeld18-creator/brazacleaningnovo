What Is Included in Maid Service in Central Florida? (Complete 2026 Breakdown)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-23·10 min read

One of the most common questions from new maid service clients: what exactly do they clean? The answer varies by company — and knowing what's standard versus what costs extra helps you budget accurately and set clear expectations from day one.

Standard Maid Service Scope — Room by Room

Every professional maid service in Central Florida should cover the following on every recurring visit.

Bedrooms

  • All surfaces dusted — nightstands, dressers, shelves, furniture tops
  • Carpets vacuumed, hard floors mopped
  • Mirrors cleaned streak-free
  • Beds made and linens straightened
  • Ceiling fans dusted (deep clean visits)

Bathrooms

  • Toilet: inside bowl, under rim, exterior, seat, lid — fully scrubbed
  • Sink and counter fully wiped and dried
  • Shower walls and glass scrubbed
  • Tub scrubbed if present
  • Mirror cleaned streak-free
  • Floor mopped
  • Trash emptied and liner replaced

Kitchen

  • All countertops wiped and dried
  • Stovetop cleaned including burners and grates
  • Sink scrubbed and dried
  • Microwave interior cleaned
  • All appliance exteriors wiped (fridge, dishwasher, toaster, coffee maker)
  • Cabinet exteriors wiped
  • Trash emptied

Living and Dining Areas

  • All surfaces dusted — tables, shelves, entertainment unit
  • TV screen wiped
  • Sofa cushions straightened
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Dining table wiped and chairs straightened

Throughout the Home

  • Light switches and door handles wiped
  • Trash emptied from all rooms
  • Stairs vacuumed if present

What Is NOT Included in Standard Maid Service

Standard recurring maid service in Central Florida does not include:

  • Inside the oven — requires specific degreasers and extended time
  • Inside the refrigerator — removing shelves and deep cleaning the interior is a separate task
  • Interior window cleaning — significant time and equipment
  • Laundry — washing, drying, folding is a separate service
  • Garage floors — available as add-on
  • Pool decks and screened lanais — outdoor add-ons
  • Inside all kitchen cabinets — deep clean task
  • Dishes or hand washing
  • Organizing or decluttering

Available Add-Ons in Central Florida

  • Inside oven: +$25–$45
  • Inside refrigerator: +$20–$40
  • Laundry (wash, dry, fold): +$15–$30/load
  • Interior window cleaning: +$50–$120
  • Lanai/screened enclosure: +$30–$55
  • Pool deck: +$30–$55
  • Inside all kitchen cabinets: +$40–$80
  • Ceiling fan deep cleaning: +$5–$10 per fan

Deep Clean vs Standard Recurring — The Difference

The first visit is a deep clean, adding to the standard scope:

  • Inside all appliances
  • Inside all kitchen and bathroom cabinets
  • Grout scrubbing in all bathrooms
  • Full baseboard washing
  • Window sills and tracks
  • Behind and under accessible furniture

Deep cleaning costs 50–70% more than a standard visit but sets a clean baseline. After it, recurring visits are faster and cheaper.

How to Set Clear Expectations

  • Walk through with your team on the first visit — show your priorities
  • Specify product preferences (no bleach, unscented, hardwood-safe)
  • Identify fragile items or off-limits areas upfront
  • Give feedback within 24 hours if anything was missed

FAQ: What Is Included in Maid Service

Does maid service include changing bed sheets?

Standard service includes making beds with existing linens. Full linen changes are an add-on, typically $5–$15 per bedroom.

Do maid service teams bring their own supplies?

Yes — professional services provide all cleaning products and equipment. Notify them of product preferences before the first visit.

How do I request add-ons?

Contact your service at least 48 hours before the visit — add-ons require additional scheduled time.
