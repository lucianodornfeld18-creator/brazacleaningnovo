Airbnb Cleaning Checklist for Kissimmee Vacation Rentals: What Guests Actually Inspect (2026)

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

A low cleanliness rating on Airbnb in Kissimmee doesn't just hurt your pride — it directly drops your search ranking and booking rate in one of the most competitive vacation rental markets in the United States. Most cleanliness complaints come from a handful of specific areas that are easy to miss in a rushed turnover. This checklist covers what guests actually inspect first, what triggers the most negative reviews in Kissimmee vacation rentals, and how a professional turnover should be structured from entry to final walkthrough.

What Kissimmee Guests Check First (The High-Priority Areas)

Guests arriving at a Kissimmee vacation rental after a long drive or flight from the airport do an immediate unconscious inspection. These are the areas they notice in the first 5 minutes:

  • Bathroom condition — specifically the toilet, mirror, and whether towels are freshly placed. A single hair on the bathroom counter or a streaky mirror is enough to set a negative first impression.
  • Bed presentation — are the beds made hotel-style with tight corners? Are the pillows arranged? Is there visible pet hair on any surface?
  • Kitchen counters — clean countertops and an empty sink signal that the home was professionally cleaned, not just tidied.
  • Smell — any pet odor, musty smell, or cleaning chemical overload registers immediately on entry. A neutral, fresh scent is the goal.
  • Pool deck — families heading straight to the pool after arrival notice immediately whether the deck has been swept and furniture arranged.

Complete Room-by-Room Kissimmee Vacation Rental Cleaning Checklist

Bedrooms (All)

  • Linens changed and beds made hotel-style with tight corners
  • All pillowcases changed, decorative pillows arranged
  • All surfaces (nightstands, dressers, furniture) wiped
  • Mirrors cleaned — no streaks
  • Closet checked for guest belongings left behind
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Ceiling fans dusted (deep clean)
  • Blinds dusted (deep clean)

Themed Kids' Rooms (Disney-Area Standard)

  • All beds made correctly per the theme (twin configurations, character bedding staged precisely)
  • Character decor items dusted and reset to listing photography position
  • Any stuffed animals or character accessories wiped and arranged
  • Extra attention to floors — kids' rooms accumulate more debris

Bathrooms (All)

  • Toilet: inside bowl, under rim, seat, lid, and exterior base — fully scrubbed
  • Sink and counter: completely wiped and dried
  • Mirror: cleaned streak-free
  • Shower and/or tub: scrubbed, grout checked, glass doors wiped
  • Floor mopped
  • Fresh towels placed in hotel-fold presentation
  • All amenities restocked (shampoo, conditioner, soap, toilet paper)
  • Trash emptied and liner replaced

Kitchen

  • All countertops wiped and dried
  • Stovetop cleaned — burners, grates, and surrounding surfaces
  • Sink scrubbed and dried
  • Dishwasher completely emptied and reset
  • All appliance exteriors wiped (fridge, microwave, toaster, coffee maker)
  • Microwave interior cleaned
  • Coffee maker cleaned and reset with fresh filter
  • Trash emptied and liner replaced
  • Cabinet exteriors wiped (deep clean: interior)

Living and Dining Areas

  • All surfaces dusted and wiped (tables, shelves, entertainment unit)
  • TV screen wiped
  • Sofa cushions checked, fluffed, and reset
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Throw blankets folded and arranged
  • Dining table wiped and chairs straightened

Pool Deck and Outdoor Areas

  • Deck swept — leaves, debris, insects
  • All outdoor furniture wiped (chairs, table, loungers)
  • Pool toys organized and stored neatly
  • Outdoor trash emptied
  • BBQ grill exterior wiped (grill interior is a separate deep clean service)

Game Room

  • All surfaces wiped including game tables and equipment
  • Controllers and remotes sanitized
  • Arcade or game machines verified functional
  • Floors cleaned

The 10 Spots That Cause the Most Negative Airbnb Reviews in Kissimmee

  1. Hair in the shower drain or on the bathroom counter
  2. Toilet not fully scrubbed under the rim
  3. Pet hair on furniture or floors (even from previous guests)
  4. Microwave interior with food splatter
  5. Dishwasher not fully emptied
  6. Pool deck not swept with furniture left scattered
  7. Beds not made hotel-style — guests can tell when sheets were just thrown on
  8. Mirrors with streaks or smudges
  9. Trash left in bins (especially outdoor bins)
  10. Odor — even a faint one from previous guests or pets

FAQ: Airbnb Cleaning Checklist for Kissimmee

What do Airbnb guests in Kissimmee complain about most?

The most common cleanliness complaints in Kissimmee vacation rentals involve bathrooms (hair, toilet condition), pet odors, and pool decks not being properly cleaned. These are all preventable with a structured professional turnover.

Should I provide my cleaning service with a checklist?

Yes — sharing this checklist with your professional cleaning service ensures nothing is missed and gives you a clear basis for follow-up if something doesn't meet standard. Most professional services have their own checklist; combining them ensures your specific property priorities are covered.

How do I maintain a 5-star cleanliness rating on Airbnb in Kissimmee?

Consistent professional turnover cleaning, deep cleans every 4–6 weeks, photo documentation after each turnover, and promptly addressing any guest feedback about cleanliness. A single 3-star cleanliness review can take 8–10 positive reviews to recover from in terms of algorithm impact.
