House Cleaning Services in Clermont FL: What to Expect and What It Costs in 2026

By Braza Cleaning Team·2026-04-07·10 min read

Clermont FL has grown rapidly — from a quiet Lake County lakeside town into one of Central Florida's most desirable places to live. The hills, the chain of lakes, the proximity to Orlando without being in it — Clermont attracts families, athletes, retirees, and remote workers who value space and quality of life. That growth has brought more house cleaning services into the market, which makes choosing the right one harder, not easier. This guide covers what professional house cleaning actually costs in Clermont FL in 2026, what good service looks like, and how to make a choice you won't have to revisit in three months.

House Cleaning Prices in Clermont FL (2026)

Current market rates for professional house cleaning in Clermont and surrounding Lake County communities (Minneola, Groveland, Montverde, Horizon West border areas):

  • 2-bedroom / 2-bath home (standard recurring): $120–$178 per visit
  • 3-bedroom / 2-bath home (standard recurring): $130–$200 per visit
  • 4-bedroom / 3-bath home (standard recurring): $150–$220 per visit
  • One-time or first clean (2BR): $130–$195
  • One-time or first clean (3BR): $165–$245
  • Deep cleaning (3BR): $240–$370
  • Move-out cleaning (3BR): $335–$480

Clermont pricing is slightly lower than Winter Garden and Winter Park to the east, reflecting Lake County's cost of living versus Orange County. The gap isn't huge — typically $10–$30 per visit — but it's consistent across service providers.

What Drives Cleaning Costs Up in Clermont

Several Clermont-specific factors affect cleaning pricing:

  • Newer, larger homes — South Lake and the SR-50 corridor feature newer builds averaging 2,200–3,000+ sq ft with open-concept layouts and 3 full bathrooms, which take longer than older compact homes near downtown Clermont
  • Pools — screened pool enclosures are common in Clermont; pool deck cleaning is typically an add-on of $30–$55
  • Pets — Clermont's outdoor lifestyle culture means many households have dogs; pet hair on tile, carpet, and furniture adds 20–30 minutes per visit
  • First-visit condition — if a home hasn't been professionally cleaned in 6+ months, expect to pay a higher rate for the initial visit before transitioning to standard recurring pricing

Types of House Cleaning Services Available in Clermont

  • Standard recurring cleaning — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Covers all living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, and kitchen surfaces. Most Clermont households book bi-weekly.
  • Deep cleaning — includes everything in standard cleaning plus inside appliances, inside cabinets, grout scrubbing, baseboards, window sills and tracks. Recommended before starting recurring service and every 6 months.
  • Move-in / move-out cleaning — comprehensive cleaning of every surface in an empty or near-empty home, designed for real estate transfers or tenant changes. Clermont's active real estate market keeps this service in consistent demand.
  • One-time cleaning — for special occasions, before or after events, or for homeowners who don't want a recurring schedule. Priced 15–25% higher per visit than equivalent recurring rates.

Bi-Weekly vs. Monthly Cleaning in Clermont: Which Makes More Sense?

Bi-weekly (every two weeks) is the most popular frequency among Clermont homeowners — it balances cleanliness and cost effectively. Monthly makes more sense for:

  • Smaller households (1–2 people) who stay on top of daily tidying
  • Seasonal residents or those who travel frequently
  • Homes under 1,200 sq ft with low accumulation rates

The price difference between bi-weekly and monthly in Clermont is relatively small — about $15–$30 per visit — but bi-weekly homes are dramatically easier to maintain at a high standard because there's less buildup between visits.

What a Standard Clermont House Cleaning Includes

  • Bedrooms: dusting, vacuuming or mopping floors, surfaces wiped, mirrors cleaned
  • Bathrooms: full toilet scrub, sink and counter wiped, shower/tub scrubbed, mirror cleaned, floor mopped
  • Kitchen: countertops, stovetop, sink, appliance exteriors, microwave interior
  • Living and dining areas: surfaces dusted, floors vacuumed/mopped

Add-ons (priced separately): inside oven, inside refrigerator, interior windows, pool deck, laundry folding, garage floor, ceiling fans.

How to Choose a Cleaning Service in Clermont FL

  • Licensed and insured — always verify before booking
  • Flat-rate pricing — know your cost before they arrive, not after
  • Consistent assigned team — the same cleaners every visit is worth prioritizing
  • Local references or reviews — a company that regularly serves Clermont will have recognizable community reviews
  • Satisfaction guarantee — a company that will re-clean anything you're not happy with at no charge

FAQ: House Cleaning in Clermont FL

How much does house cleaning cost in Clermont FL in 2026?

House cleaning in Clermont FL averages $120–$178 per visit for a 2-bedroom home and $130–$200 for a 3-bedroom on a recurring bi-weekly schedule. One-time and deep cleaning visits cost more.

Is bi-weekly or monthly cleaning better for Clermont homeowners?

Bi-weekly is the most popular choice in Clermont and delivers better results — less buildup means more effective cleaning on each visit. Monthly works well for smaller households or frequent travelers.

Do Clermont cleaning services include pool deck cleaning?

Most standard house cleaning services in Clermont don't include pool deck cleaning by default — it's typically available as an add-on ranging from $30–$55. Confirm this when getting your quote.

How do I know if I need a deep clean before starting recurring service?

If the home hasn't been professionally cleaned in 3+ months, has significant buildup in the kitchen or bathrooms, or has a first-visit condition that's notably below standard, a deep clean before starting recurring service is recommended. Most professional Clermont cleaners will assess this during an initial quote visit.
