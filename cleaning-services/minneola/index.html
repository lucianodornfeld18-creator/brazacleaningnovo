OUR PROCESS

The Braza 57-Point Cleaning Checklist

Every clean follows all 57 points — nothing is optional, nothing is skipped.

🛏️

Bedrooms

12 points

  1. Change & make all beds with fresh linens
  2. Dust all furniture tops & surfaces
  3. Wipe nightstands, lamps & headboards
  4. Clean mirrors streak-free
  5. Vacuum carpet or mop hard floors
  6. Check under beds for lost items
  7. Empty & wipe trash cans
  8. Dust ceiling fans & light fixtures
  9. Wipe baseboards & door frames
  10. Check closets & organize if needed
  11. Inspect for damage & missing items
  12. Final walk-through + photo documentation
🚿

Bathrooms

13 points

  1. Scrub & disinfect toilet inside & out
  2. Clean & shine sink and faucets
  3. Scrub shower/tub — grout & tiles
  4. Clean shower glass streak-free
  5. Polish mirrors & chrome fixtures
  6. Wipe vanity & counter surfaces
  7. Mop & disinfect floor
  8. Replace towels with fresh set
  9. Restock toiletries & toilet paper
  10. Empty & sanitize trash can
  11. Wipe light switches & door handles
  12. Check for mold & soap residue
  13. Deodorize & ventilate
🍳

Kitchen

14 points

  1. Wipe all countertops & backsplash
  2. Clean stovetop — burners & grates
  3. Wipe outside of oven & microwave
  4. Clean inside microwave
  5. Wipe refrigerator outside & handles
  6. Check & clean refrigerator inside
  7. Wash, dry & put away dishes
  8. Wipe cabinet fronts & handles
  9. Clean & sanitize sink
  10. Degrease exhaust fan/hood
  11. Empty & sanitize trash cans
  12. Sweep & mop floors
  13. Restock dish soap & sponge
  14. Check for expired food & remove
🛋️

Living Areas

10 points

  1. Dust all surfaces, shelves & decor
  2. Wipe TV screens & remotes
  3. Clean glass tables & windows
  4. Fluff & arrange sofa cushions
  5. Vacuum upholstery & rugs
  6. Sweep & mop hard floors
  7. Wipe light switches & outlets
  8. Dust baseboards & skirting
  9. Empty trash cans
  10. Final walkthrough & client notification
✅

Final Check

8 points

  1. Full property walk-through
  2. Photo documentation — every room
  3. Damage & missing items report
  4. Completion notification to client
  5. Lock & secure property
  6. WhatsApp confirmation sent
  7. Client confirmation received
  8. Quality score logged internally
