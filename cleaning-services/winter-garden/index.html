OUR PROCESS

The Braza 57-Point Cleaning Checklist

Every Braza clean follows a rigorous 57-point checklist. Our trained team covers every corner — so you never have to check behind us.

🛏️ Bedrooms (12 points)

  1. Change linens or make beds neatly
  2. Dust all furniture and surfaces
  3. Wipe nightstands, lamps, and headboards
  4. Clean mirrors streak-free
  5. Vacuum carpet or mop hard floors
  6. Check under beds
  7. Empty and wipe trash cans
  8. Dust ceiling fans and fixtures
  9. Wipe baseboards and door frames
  10. Organize visible clutter
  11. Wipe light switches and outlets
  12. Final check and photo if requested

🚿 Bathrooms (13 points)

  1. Scrub and disinfect toilet
  2. Clean sink and faucets
  3. Scrub shower/tub and tiles
  4. Clean shower glass
  5. Polish mirrors and chrome
  6. Wipe vanity and surfaces
  7. Mop and disinfect floor
  8. Fold towels neatly
  9. Restock toilet paper
  10. Empty and sanitize trash
  11. Wipe light switches
  12. Check for mold and soap buildup
  13. Deodorize

🍳 Kitchen (14 points)

  1. Wipe all countertops
  2. Clean stovetop and burners
  3. Wipe outside of appliances
  4. Clean inside microwave
  5. Wipe refrigerator exterior
  6. Wash and put away dishes
  7. Wipe cabinet fronts
  8. Clean and sanitize sink
  9. Degrease exhaust hood
  10. Empty and sanitize trash
  11. Sweep and mop floors
  12. Wipe backsplash
  13. Wipe light switches
  14. Organize visible counter items

🛋️ Living Areas + Final (18 points)

  1. Dust all surfaces and shelves
  2. Wipe TV and electronics
  3. Clean glass surfaces
  4. Fluff sofa cushions
  5. Vacuum upholstery and rugs
  6. Sweep and mop floors
  7. Wipe switches and outlets
  8. Dust baseboards
  9. Empty trash cans
  10. Vacuum stairs if applicable
  11. Clean front door exterior
  12. Wipe door handles throughout
  13. Check laundry area
  14. Wipe washer/dryer exterior
  15. Spot-clean walls if needed
  16. Full property walk-through
  17. Lock and secure home
  18. Confirmation sent to client
