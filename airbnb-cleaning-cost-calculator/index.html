What changes the price of a house cleaning

  • Home size — square footage, bedrooms and bathrooms drive the base price.
  • Cleaning type — standard vs deep clean vs move-out (deep is 1.5–2x).
  • Condition & frequency — heavily-soiled homes cost more; recurring saves 15–20%.
  • Pets — extra hair and dander add time.
  • Add-ons — inside oven/fridge, windows, laundry and baseboards.

What drives the price up or down

Two homes with the same bedroom count can have very different turnover costs. Here's what moves the number.

Size & bathrooms

Bedrooms, bathrooms and total square footage are the biggest factors. More beds to make and bathrooms to sanitize means more labor per turn.

Market & resort community

Premium communities like Reunion, ChampionsGate and Celebration carry higher rates than standard residential areas, reflecting access, standards and demand.

Add-ons

Laundry of linens and towels, pet hair, hot tub, pool deck, smoke/odor and restocking consumables each add to the base turnover fee.

Turnaround & condition

Same-day checkout-to-check-in rush turns and heavily-soiled checkouts take priority scheduling and extra time, which is reflected in the quote.
