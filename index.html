About Braza Cleaning Services

One of Central Florida's Most Trusted Cleaning Services

Braza Cleaning Services is a licensed, bonded, and fully insured professional cleaning company serving homeowners, vacation rental hosts, property managers, and businesses throughout Central Florida. We are a Brazilian-owned small business operating in the Orlando metro area, founded on the Brazilian tradition of meticulous attention to detail and genuine care for every client's home and property.

We specialize in two distinct markets: residential house cleaning for homeowners and active adult communities throughout Lake, Sumter, and Marion counties (including The Villages, Clermont, Winter Garden, and Minneola); and vacation rental turnover cleaning for Airbnb and VRBO hosts throughout Osceola and Polk counties (including Kissimmee, Davenport, Four Corners, and Celebration).
